# 🛒 Superstore Sales Dashboard
### Power BI | Data Analytics Project

![Dashboard](screenshot(99).png)

---

## 📌 Problem Statement
A retail company wants to understand their sales performance 
across regions, categories, and time to make smarter 
business decisions and reduce losses.

**Key Business Questions:**
- Which region generates maximum profit?
- Which product categories are losing money?
- What is the monthly & yearly profit trend?
- Which shipping mode is most preferred?
- How do payment modes impact orders?

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights
- ✅ 2-Page Interactive Dashboard
- ✅ Region Filter — Central / East / South / West
- ✅ KPI Cards — Sales, Orders, Profit, Avg Ship Days
- ✅ Profit by Month & Year Trend
- ✅ Sales by Category, Sub-Category & Segment  
- ✅ Ship Mode & Payment Mode Analysis
- ✅ State-wise Sales Map (USA)

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights
1. 📱 **Technology** is the highest revenue generating category
2. 🪑 **Furniture** has lowest profit margin among all categories
3. 🚚 **Standard Class** is the most preferred shipping mode (55%)
4. 💳 **COD** is most used payment mode (44%)
5. 🏆 **Consumer segment** drives 54% of total sales
6. 📅 **Q4 months** show consistent profit spike every year
